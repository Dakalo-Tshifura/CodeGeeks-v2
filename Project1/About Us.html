About Mamali

The name of the school is Mamali Sikhwivhilu Primary, located in Ngovhela Limpopo, a semi-township. The school is currently under headmaster Mrs Siaga, it has recently launched a library hub in the year 2022. The school was initially created for primary school students from grade 1 to grade 7, but due to the rapid population growth (births and migration) the school is also enrolling higher grades. Students are allowed up to grade 12 - which is the matric year.

  • Our Vision

    Imparting education of global standards that combines both the traditional and the modern ideals., in order to develop well rounded, confident and responsible individuals who aspire to achieve their full potential.

  • Our Mission

    Dispensing their duties & to be conscious of their responsibilities towards their fellow citizens, thus fostering a Lifelong Love of Reading & Learning: Through curriculum and enrichment activities
